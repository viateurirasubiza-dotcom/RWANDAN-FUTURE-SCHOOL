# About RWANDAN FUTURE MIS RWANDAN FUTURE MIS is a modern School Management Information System designed to help schools in Rwanda manage academic and administrative activities efficiently. The system enables schools to manage students, teachers, marks, attendance, report cards, and school records digitally. It aims to improve accuracy, reduce paperwork, and support better decision-making through technology. ### Key Features * Student Management * Teacher Management * Marks and Assessment Recording * Automated Report Cards * Attendance Tracking * School Administration Tools * Secure Cloud Data Storage * Real-Time Updates ### Mission To provide affordable and innovative digital solutions that improve education management in Rwanda and beyond. ### Developer RWANDAN FUTURE MIS was developed by IRASUBIZA VIATEUR, a student of Mathematics and Computer Science at East African University Rwanda. ### Contact Email: [viateurirasubiza@gmail.com](mailto:viateurirasubiza@gmail.com) Phone: +250 794 476 085
