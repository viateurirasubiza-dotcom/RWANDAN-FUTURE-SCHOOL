About RWANDAN FUTURE MIS

Smart School Management System for Rwanda Education

RWANDAN FUTURE MIS is a modern School Management Information System designed to help schools in Rwanda manage academic and administrative activities efficiently.

The system enables schools to manage students, teachers, marks, attendance, report cards, and school records digitally. It aims to improve accuracy, reduce paperwork, and support better decision-making through technology.

Key Features

  • Student Management
  • Teacher Management
  • Marks and Assessment Recording
  • Automated Report Cards
  • Attendance Tracking
  • School Administration Tools
  • Secure Cloud Data Storage
  • Real-Time Updates

Mission

To provide affordable and innovative digital solutions that improve education management in Rwanda and beyond.
